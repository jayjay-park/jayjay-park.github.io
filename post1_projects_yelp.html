Project Description

This project's purpose was to visualize the distribution of the reviews from the Yelp public dataset in order to gain insight on the unstructured dataset. Followings are the approach I used for the visualization.

  • Library used: ggplot2
  • Method used: unsupervised method (k-means)
I used ggplot2 library for the visualization, and used k-means function for clustering the data in order to get an insight on the distribution of the data. Specifically, I visualized the relationship between the rate each business received and the number of reviews that business received in total. Through visualization, I was able observe that business with positive reviews tended to receive higher number of reviews when business with negative reviews tended to receive lower number of reviews.
