N-CATS: Neural Chaotic Auto-Correlation for Time Series

Inspired by a recent discovery that the cryptocurrency market is a chaotic system, to learn dynamics from data with high volatility and noise, let's use chaos theory in training the model.


Motivation

Due to high volatility, predicting cryptocurrency price is a challenging problem. But recent discoveries claim that the cryptocurrency market shows chaotic behavior. Thus, let's use that as prior knowledge and feed it to neural network!

Identify Research Gap

1. To the best of my knowledge, as of right now, no existing model learns both data trend + market dynamics for cryptocurrency-price prediction

2. Also, no existing model is trained with information on chaotic behavior of cryptocurrency market

Research Questions

1. Can we verify if the cryptocurrency market is indeed chaotic?
2. How do baseline models perform in predicting price?
3. How can we make a model learn market dynamics information?
4. How does the new model perform?

Method

Data:

  • Kaggle Bitcoin Historical Dataset.
  • The Dataset includes every 1 minute historical price in 2021. Only used closing price, as a problem of interest is a univariate time series prediction.

Baseline:

  • 1. LSTM
  • 2. Neural ODE (Neural Ordinary Differential Equations)

Result

Multi-step prediction of N-CATS



1. Was able to verify if crypto-currency market is indeed chaotic as claimed by previous works
2. Devised a new training algorithm for N-CATS
3. Found out that LSTM does not perform well in both short term and long term prediction with limited data. Neural ODE performs well in short term prediction but performs poorly in long term prediction.
4. N-CATS outperform industry standards(LSTM) and baseline(Neural ODE) by performing great in both short term and long term prediction.
